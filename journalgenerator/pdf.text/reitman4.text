Abstract

equations coupled with the heat equation for the Stefan problem in 3-dimensional
space describing a microwave heating process. F or this problem, a multi-v alued
dynamical system is constructed and the existence of a global attractor is proved.
A numerical experiment for the temperature approximation is carried out.
