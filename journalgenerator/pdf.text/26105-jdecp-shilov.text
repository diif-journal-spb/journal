Investigation of Boundedness of Solutions to a Model System of
Ordinary Di erential Equations
Shilov K.V., Naimov A.N.
V ologda State University
shilovkv@vogu35.ru
naimov an@vogu35.ru
Abstract. W e investigate the question of right-boundedness of solutions for a
model system of ordinary di erential equations and their approach to bounded
solutions. A theorem on the dissipativity of the considered system of equations
and the existence of bounded solutions is formulated and proved. Conditions
ensuring the dissipativity of the system are based on a one-sided estimate of the
coe cient matrix. Under dissipativity conditions, the existence of stationary and
periodic solutions is proved when the right-hand sides of the system of equations
are autonomous or periodic. In the case of a constant coe cient matrix, conditions
equiv alent to the one-sided estimate are provided.
Keywords: model system of equations, dissipative system, bounded solution,
stationary solution, periodic solution, one-sided estimate.
