Local topological equiv alence of essentially nonlinear systems of
di erential equations.
Y u.A. Iljin, K.V. Martynov
St. Petersburg State University
iljin_y_a@mail.ru, martynovkiry@gmail.com.
Abstract. The authors examine a system of nonlinear homogeneous di eren-
tial equations and its perturbation by higher-order terms. The aim is to  nd
conditions under which the original system and its perturbed version are locally
topologically equiv alent in the neighborhood of the zero equilibrium point.
The paper is based on the approach proposed by E. Reizin in 1971. But
instead of using W azewski’s coe cient condition employed by him, the authors
apply Lozinskii logarithmic norms, which made it possible to signi cantly ex-
pand the class of equiv alent systems. Lozinskii norms also represent a coe cient
condition, which is particularly important for applied problems.
Keywords: topological equiv alence, essentially nonlinear system, logarithmic
norm.
