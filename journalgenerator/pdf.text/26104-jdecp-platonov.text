Stability analysis for non-stationary complex systems with
switching and impulsive e ects
Platonov A.V.
Saint Petersburg State University
a.platonov@spbu.ru
Abstract. The paper investigates the stability problem for a class of nonlinear
non-stationary complex systems. It is assumed that the system is sub ject to
switching and impulsive e ects. Di erent approaches to establishing the conditions
of the asymptotic stability of a given equilibrium position are proposed. These
conditions link the constraints on the switching/impulse law and on the rate of
change of the non-stationary parameters of the system. Note that the described
approaches are applicable under broad assumptions about non-stationarities
present in the system. In particular, the coe cients of the system can be
unbounded with respect to time or, conversely , inseparable from zero. Finding
the required conditions for the asymptotic stability is based on the Lyapunov
function method. A numerical example is given to illustrate the application of
the proved theorems. The results obtained can be used in the framework of the
problem of the stability analysis of non-stationary systems under nonlinear hybrid
approximation.
Key words: non-stationary complex systems, asymptotic stability , switching,
impulsive e ects.
