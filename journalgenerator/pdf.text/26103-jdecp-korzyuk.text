Abstract. We consider the Cauchy problem for a mildly quasilinear wave
equationwithavariablecoefficientintheupperhalf-plane. Weusethemethod
of characteristics to construct the solution in an implicit analytical form as a
solution of an integro-differential equation. We study the solvability of this
equation, as well as the dependence on the initial data and the smoothness
of its solutions. We prove the uniqueness of the solution for the problem in
question and establish the conditions under which its classical solution exists.
Keywords: mildly quasilinear wave equation, Cauchy problem, classical solu-
tion, apriori estimates, parameter continuation method.

6 Conclusions
In this paper, we derive the necessary and sufficient conditions under which
there exists a unique classical solution of the Cauchy problem in the upper
half-plane for a mildly quasilinear wave equation with a variable coefficient.
We also established the dependence of the smoothness of the solution on the
smoothnessoftheinitialdata. Inthefuture,weplantousetheresultsobtained
