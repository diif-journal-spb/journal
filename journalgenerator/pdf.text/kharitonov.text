Abstract
The problem of computing of the norm of transfer matrices of neutral type
time-delay systems is considered. It is known, that the Lyapunov matrices can
be used to compute the norm of delay free linear time invariant systems. The
obtained result is similar to this one. It is shown, that the norm of the transfer
matrix of a neutral type time-delay system can be expressed in the terms of the
Lyapunov matrices for time-delay systems. In this work the explicit expression
for the norm is received.Дифференциальные уравнения и процессы управления,N. 4, 2014
