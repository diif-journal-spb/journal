Abstract. In this paper, we consider a class of nonlinear V olterra integro-
di erential equations with a logarithmic kernel. The study is conducted in a
family of weighted spaces on a  nite interv al with power-law singularities at
its endpoints. Such integro-di erential equations arise in the study of v arious
physical phenomena. F or example, such equations are obtained when considering
the problem of stellar brightness  uctuations; when studying physical phenomena
containing some memories; when studying the aerodynamics of an aircraft wing,
and in many other phenomena.
The problem of constructing a conformal mapping of the unit circle onto a
stellar domain is reduced to solving a nonlinear singular integral equation with
a logarithmic kernel. The study of many degenerate partial di erential equations
is associated with the study of the corresponding degenerate ordinary di erential
equations. It is enough to mention here numerous works on the Euler equation,
the Bessel polynomial, the Euler gamma and beta functions, the Euler and
Gauss hypergeometric functions, the works of Abel, Jacobi, and W eierstrass on
elliptic functions, and others. Many of these functions were introduced to solve
speci c problems, namely , singular problems. The study of di erential equations
with di erent types of singularity was begun in the works of Euler, Poisson,
and Darboux, continued in the theory of generalized axisymmetric potential
by A. W einstein and L. Bers, and continued in the works of many modern
mathematicians.
The article presents the solution representation of nonlinear integro-
di erential equation with logarithmic kernel and with v arious degrees of
nonlinearity .
