nonlinear integro-di erential equations with a zero di erential operator, a rapidly

regularized using the spectrum of the limit operator of the equiv alent di erential
system. It is shown that nonlinearity plays a signi cant role in identifying
essentially singular points in the solution of this problem. In the presence
of only internal resonance between the eigenv alues ??of a rapidly oscillating
inhomogeneity and the absence of external resonance with the spectral v alue of the
integral operator, essentially singular points are identi ed by the spectral v alue
and spectrum of the oscillating inhomogeneity . An algorithm for constructing
regularized (according to Lomov) asymptotic solutions is developed. The existence
of such an asymptotic behavior is discussed, since in the presence of an arbitrary
nonlinearity in the problem, such an asymptotic behavior may not exist, which is
clearly demonstrated in the example given in the paper.
